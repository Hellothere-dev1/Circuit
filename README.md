# Circuit Craft & Assembly Log

This repository documents the hands-on hardware progress of our STEM learning journey. It focuses on the transition from temporary prototyping (breadboarding) to permanent hardware assembly (soldering).

---

## 🔧 Core Skills Tracking
* **Breadboarding:** Understanding circuit paths, power rails, and component placement for rapid testing.
* **Soldering (PTH):** Mastering Through-Hole Technology, heat management, and creating clean solder joints.
* **Component Literacy:** Identifying and utilizing resistors, capacitors, transistors, LEDs, and integrated circuits (ICs).
* **Hardware Debugging:** Using multimeters to test continuity, voltage drops, and troubleshooting "short circuits."

---

## 🛠 Tools & Materials
* **Prototyping:** Half-size/Full-size breadboards, jumper wire kits.
* **Assembly:** Temperature-controlled soldering station, lead-free solder, flux, and desoldering pump.
* **Testing:** Digital Multimeter (DMM), DC Power Supply.
